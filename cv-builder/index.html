Frequently asked questions

Is my resume really ATS-compatible?

−
Yes. All CV Builder templates are specifically designed to pass ATS parsing. We avoid complex formatting, unusual fonts, and visual elements that break in ATS systems. Your resume will be readable by both machines and humans.

Is CV Builder really free?

+
Yes, you can build and export your first resume completely free. Premium includes access to all 20+ templates, unlimited exports, and the cover letter builder. You choose when to upgrade.

Can I import my old resume?

+
Absolutely. You can upload your existing resume or CV, and CV Builder will help you update and reformat it into a new template. No need to start from scratch.

What file formats can I download?

+
You can export your resume as PDF (most common and safe), JPG (for sharing as an image), or PNG (with transparency option). All formats maintain your formatting and layout.

Can I create multiple versions of my resume?

+
Yes. With premium, you can create unlimited resumes. This is helpful for tailoring your resume to different jobs or industries.

Can I edit fonts and colors?

+
Yes, fully customizable. Change fonts, colors, spacing, and layout to match your personal brand. Our defaults are ATS-safe, and customization stays that way.

Does CV Builder offer cover letters?

+
Yes, the cover letter builder creates letters that match your resume's design and tone. Available with premium. Quickly tailor cover letters to specific jobs.
