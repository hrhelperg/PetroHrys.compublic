Frequently asked questions

Is Unzip really free?
▼
Yes. The base app is free with all core features — extract ZIP files, preview archives, and manage files. Premium unlocks unlimited extraction, compression, and password protection for all formats. Most users never need premium.
Does Unzip upload my files?
▼
No. Everything happens on your phone. Unzip never sends files to cloud servers or third parties. Your privacy is always protected. Check our Privacy Policy to see exactly what data we collect (spoiler: almost nothing).
Can I extract RAR files on iOS?
▼
Yes, that's the whole point. The iOS Files app doesn't support RAR or 7Z, but Unzip does. Download it, and you'll unlock all the archive formats your phone was missing.
What formats does Unzip support?
▼
Unzip extracts ZIP, RAR, 7Z, TAR, and GZ files. You can also create ZIP archives and compress files to share. If you need a format we don't support yet, let us know.
Can I compress files with password protection?
▼
Yes. Create a ZIP, add a password, and share it securely. Only people with the password can extract the files. Perfect for sending sensitive documents.
Is Unzip fast enough for large files?
▼
Yes. Unzip is optimized for speed and runs entirely on your device. Large archives extract in seconds without stuttering. The app itself is lightweight and won't slow down your phone.
