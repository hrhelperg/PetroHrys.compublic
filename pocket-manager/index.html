Frequently asked questions

Is my data secure and private? ▼

Yes. All your data stays on your device. We never sync to external servers, never see your financial information, and never share data with third parties. Your budget is completely private.

Do I need to connect my bank account? ▼

No. Unlike many budget apps, Pocket Manager doesn't require or support bank account linking. You manually log your expenses, which gives you more control and better privacy.

How much does Pocket Manager cost? ▼

Core budget tracking is completely free. We offer a premium tier for advanced analytics and additional features, but the main app is free to use forever.

Can I use it on both iOS and Android? ▼

Yes. Pocket Manager is available on both iOS (iPhone) and Android. Since data stays on your device, you'll need to set it up separately on each phone.

What if I lose my phone? Can I recover my data? ▼

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend backing up your phone regularly using iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android). You can also export your data from the app's settings.

Can I export my data? ▼

Yes. Pocket Manager lets you export your expense data in standard formats that work with other apps and spreadsheets. Your data is yours to keep.

How often is Pocket Manager updated? ▼

We release updates regularly with bug fixes, performance improvements, and new features based on user feedback. Check the app store for the latest version.
