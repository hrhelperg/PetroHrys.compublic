Frequently asked questions

Is this a real phone number? +

Yes. You get a real, genuine local phone number that works exactly like any other phone number in that country. People can call you on it, text you, and use it for verification codes. It's a real number issued by legitimate carriers.

Do I need to download an app? +

No. TwinPhone works entirely in your browser—Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. No installation, no SIM cards, no carrier changes. Just log in and you're ready to call.

Can I receive calls on my number? +

Yes. Inbound calls ring straight to your browser when you're logged in. You can answer immediately. If you're offline, calls go to voicemail which gets transcribed automatically.

Are calls really encrypted? +

Yes. Every call is end-to-end encrypted by default. Only you and the person you're calling can hear the conversation. We can't listen in, and neither can anyone else on the network.

What if I need a number in a country not listed? +

We support 145+ countries and keep adding more. Check the full list when you sign up. If you need a specific country, reach out—we're always expanding based on user demand.

Can I keep my number or transfer it? +

You keep your TwinPhone number as long as your account is active. If you cancel, you can reactivate it later. Number porting to other services varies by country—contact us for details.

Is there a minimum contract or commitment? +

No. Zero contracts, zero commitments. Start with a free trial (no credit card), and cancel anytime if it's not for you. No penalties, no closing fees, nothing like that.
