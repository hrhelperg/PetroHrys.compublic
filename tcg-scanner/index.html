Frequently Asked Questions

+ How accurate is the AI recognition?

Our AI is trained to recognize trading cards with high accuracy. It works with cards at different angles, lighting conditions, and even partially obscured cards. For difficult cases, you can manually verify or correct the recognition.

+ Which card games does DEX support?

DEX currently supports the most popular trading card games, with a focus on cards with active secondary markets. We're constantly expanding our database to support more games and editions.

+ Is there a free version?

Yes. DEX is free to download and use for basic card scanning and price checking. Premium features include advanced collection management and price alerts.

+ Does it work at card shops and tournaments?

Absolutely. Designed to work anywhere—shops, tournaments, flea markets, or at home. You can scan cards on display, in hands, or in binders.

+ Can I export my collection data?

Yes. You can export your collection in multiple formats for backup, insurance documentation, or use in spreadsheets.

+ Is my data secure and private?

Your collection data is stored securely. We don't sell your data or use it for advertising. See our privacy policy for complete details on how we handle your information.
