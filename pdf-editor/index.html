The PDF Problem

If you've ever needed to edit a PDF, you know the frustration. Most PDF tools come with three big problems:

Expensive subscriptions
Adobe Acrobat costs $20+ per month. For something you might use a few times a year, that's a poor deal.
Privacy concerns
Web-based PDF editors upload your documents to unknown servers. Your sensitive contracts, tax forms, and medical records shouldn't be in the cloud.
Overkill features
Enterprise PDF software includes features designed for teams and complex workflows. You just need to sign a lease or convert a file.

PDF Editor solves this. It's a free app that works entirely on your device. No subscriptions, no uploads, no complicated toolbars. Just the 90% of PDF features that most people actually need, available offline, whenever you need it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Editor really free? +
Yes. PDF Editor is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium tiers. All features are available to every user. We believe essential tools should be free and accessible to everyone.
Do my documents get uploaded to a server? +
No. PDF Editor processes everything on your device locally. Your documents never leave your phone or tablet. This is a core design principle—we don't have servers that store your files, and we don't track what you're editing. Complete privacy.
Can I use signatures from PDF Editor on legally binding documents? +
Yes, in most jurisdictions. E-signatures created with PDF Editor are legally valid for most contracts, agreements, and forms in the US, EU, and other regions. However, some high-security situations (like certain notarized documents) may require pen-and-paper or notarized signatures. When in doubt, check your local laws or consult the document issuer.
What file formats are supported? +
PDF Editor works with PDF files natively. For conversion, it supports: DOCX (Word), XLSX (Excel), JPG, PNG, and other common image formats. You can convert from and to most of these formats. If you need support for a specific format, let us know—we're always improving.
Does it work offline? +
Yes, completely. PDF Editor works offline out of the box. You don't need an internet connection to edit, sign, convert, or scan documents. The app functions fully on airplane mode. This is another reason we don't upload documents to servers—we don't need to.
How does the OCR (scanning) feature work? +
When you scan a document, PDF Editor uses on-device optical character recognition (OCR) to extract text from the image. This means the app recognizes the text in your photo and makes it searchable and selectable in the resulting PDF. The OCR runs locally on your device—no data is sent to external servers.
What if I have a password-protected PDF? +
PDF Editor can open password-protected PDFs if you have the password. Simply enter the password when prompted. If a PDF is encrypted and you don't have the password, the app won't be able to access it—and neither would any other tool.
