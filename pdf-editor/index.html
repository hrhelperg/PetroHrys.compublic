Why PDF Tools Are Frustrating

If you've ever needed to edit a PDF on your phone, you know the drill. Most solutions have the same three problems:

Expensive subscriptions
Adobe Acrobat costs $20+ per month. For something you use a few times a year, that's a bad deal.
Privacy concerns
Web-based tools upload your documents to unknown servers. Your contracts, tax forms, and medical records shouldn't be in the cloud.
Overkill complexity
Enterprise PDF software is built for teams and complex workflows. You just need to sign a lease or convert a file.

PDF Editor solves this. It's a free app that works entirely on your device — no subscriptions, no uploads, no complicated toolbars. Just the features that most people actually need, offline, whenever you need them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Editor really free? +
Yes. PDF Editor is completely free to download and use. No hidden costs, no subscriptions, no premium tiers. All features — scanning, converting, signing, merging — are available to every user.
Can I sign PDFs on my iPhone or Android for free? +
Yes. PDF Editor lets you draw your handwritten signature or type it, then apply it to any page of any PDF — completely free. You can save your signature for reuse. Works on both iPhone and Android without any account.
Does it work offline? +
Yes, completely. PDF Editor works offline with no internet connection required. All editing, signing, converting, and scanning happens on your device. It functions fully on airplane mode.
Do my documents get uploaded to a server? +
No. PDF Editor processes everything locally on your device. Your documents never leave your phone. This is a core design principle — we don't have servers that store your files, and we don't track what you're editing. Complete privacy.
What file formats does the PDF converter support? +
PDF Editor converts to and from: DOCX (Word), XLSX (Excel), JPG, and PNG. You can convert any of these formats to PDF and export PDFs back to Word or image formats. More formats are being added.
Are e-signatures from PDF Editor legally valid? +
Yes, in most jurisdictions. E-signatures are legally valid for most contracts, agreements, and forms in the US, EU, and many other regions under laws like ESIGN and eIDAS. Certain high-security documents (notarized, court-filed) may still require wet signatures — check with the document issuer when unsure.
How does the Scan to PDF feature work? +
Open the camera scanner in PDF Editor and point it at any document. The app automatically detects edges, corrects perspective, enhances contrast, and creates a clean PDF. OCR then extracts the text so the resulting PDF is searchable — all on-device, no cloud processing.
What if I have a password-protected PDF? +
PDF Editor can open password-protected PDFs when you have the password — just enter it when prompted. Files encrypted without a known password cannot be accessed, as with any PDF tool.
