I build tools I'd want to use

I'm Petro Hrys, an app developer based between Europe and the US. I was born in Ukraine and have spent the last decade building focused, privacy-first software for iOS and Android.

I'm frustrated by bloated apps, dark patterns, and the surveillance economy. Every tool I build is designed to solve a real problem without the unnecessary complexity. I believe good software respects your time, protects your data, and doesn't waste your attention.

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring how technology can be built with genuine respect for users—not just for engagement metrics.
