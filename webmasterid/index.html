Frequently asked questions

Is WebmasterID really privacy-first? ▼

Yes. WebmasterID is cookieless by default and consent-gated — the tracker loads only after a visitor grants analytics consent.

Does it use cookies? ▼

No — WebmasterID is cookieless by default, so you can measure your audience without the usual cookie overhead.

What can I measure? ▼

Traffic sources, acquisition channels, visitor behaviour, and conversion performance — the essentials, clearly reported.

Does it work with consent managers? ▼

Yes. WebmasterID integrates with consent tools such as CookieYes, and the tracker fires only after analytics consent is granted.

How do I add it to my site? ▼

Add a single lightweight script snippet to your pages. For setup details and to get started, visit webmasterid.com.
