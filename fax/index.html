Frequently Asked Questions

+ Is it really free?

Yes. FAX is completely free to download and use. We don't charge per fax, don't require a subscription, and there are no hidden fees. Send as many faxes as you need.

+ Does it work internationally?

Yes, FAX can send to fax numbers in most countries. International fax numbers use the standard international format with the + country code.

+ How do I know the fax was delivered?

The app provides instant delivery confirmation when your fax is successfully sent. You'll see the confirmation right in the app, and you can also request a receipt if needed.

+ What file types can I send?

You can send PDFs, photos (JPG, PNG), and most document formats. The app automatically converts them to the proper fax format for transmission.

+ Do I need a fax number to send?

No. You don't need to own or have access to a fax number. Just enter the recipient's fax number and send. You're good to go.

+ Is my document secure?

Yes. Your documents are processed locally on your device before transmission. They're encrypted in transit and we don't store copies on our servers.
