Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Petro Hrys ("I", "me") builds privacy-first apps. This page is a general overview of how I handle your data. Each individual app has its own detailed privacy policy, linked from its product page and App Store listing.

General principles

All my apps are built around a simple principle: your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it. Specifically:

  • No accounts required to use any app
  • No tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or ad networks
  • No data sold to third parties
  • Files are processed locally on your device
