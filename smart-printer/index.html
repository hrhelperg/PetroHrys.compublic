Frequently Asked Questions

Will Smart Printer work with my printer? +
Smart Printer supports 10,000+ AirPrint-compatible printers from major brands like HP, Canon, Epson, and Brother. If your printer supports AirPrint, it works with Smart Printer. Check your printer's manual or manufacturer website to confirm AirPrint support.
Do I need to set anything up? +
No setup required. Smart Printer automatically discovers printers on your Wi-Fi network. Just open the app, select your file, choose a printer, and print.
Can I scan documents with Smart Printer? +
Yes. Smart Printer includes a built-in document scanner that uses your camera with automatic edge detection and optimization. Perfect for receipts, IDs, contracts, and other documents.
What file types can I print? +
Smart Printer supports PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, PPT, JPG, PNG, and TXT files. You can also print from emails, web pages, and cloud services like Dropbox and iCloud.
Can I see how my document will look before printing? +
Absolutely. Smart Printer includes a PDF preview feature so you can see exactly how your document will look before sending it to the printer.
Is my data private? +
Yes. Smart Printer takes privacy seriously. Your documents are processed locally on your device and never stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them to cloud storage.
