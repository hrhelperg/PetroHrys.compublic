Frequently Asked Questions

Is Invoice Maker really free? +
Yes. Invoice Maker is free to download and use. You can create, send, and track unlimited invoices at no cost. We offer a Pro plan for advanced features like unlimited storage, additional templates, and advanced client management.
Can I set up recurring invoices? +
Absolutely. Invoice Maker supports recurring billing for monthly retainers, subscriptions, or any repeating charge. Set it up once and the app will automatically generate and send invoices on your schedule.
Can I customize my invoices with my brand? +
Yes. You can add your business logo, choose brand colors, customize fonts, and add custom notes to every invoice. Each invoice will reflect your business identity.
Can I send invoices by email? +
Yes. Invoice Maker can email invoices directly to your clients as PDF attachments. You can also export invoices as PDFs to send through your preferred email client.
Does Invoice Maker accept payments? +
Invoice Maker helps you create and track invoices, but doesn't process payments directly. However, you can include your payment methods (bank transfer, PayPal, credit card) in custom notes on each invoice.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Invoice Maker takes privacy and security seriously. Your invoices and client data are stored securely and never shared with third parties.
